Policy & Deliverables

Clear Scope, Clear Revisions,
Clear File Support

Most self-publishing problems happen because scope and deliverables were never defined properly. We keep them explicit.

Revision Policy

Revisions are included within the approved design and formatting scope. If the project direction changes materially after approval, we clarify the added work before proceeding.

Technical File Support

If KDP or IngramSpark flags a production issue caused by our file setup, we revise the file. This does not cover metadata errors, account issues, or changes made after final delivery.

What Is Not Included

Packages do not automatically include developmental editing, paid ads, review generation, bestseller claims, or platform account management unless explicitly scoped in writing.
