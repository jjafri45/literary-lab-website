Literary Lab Studio
Who We Are

Built for
Self-Published Authors

Literary Lab was founded with one goal: to give independent authors access to the same level of professional book design that major publishers provide their authors — at prices that make sense for self-publishing budgets.

Based in Pakistan and serving clients across the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and beyond, we've helped over 200 authors bring their books to market with covers that sell and interiors that read beautifully.

Every project is handled personally by our design team. We use Adobe InDesign — the industry standard — to produce files that meet the exact specifications of every major publishing platform, so your book publishes without errors.
