Publishing Consultation

Self-Publishing Consultation
for Authors Who Need Clarity First

Many first-time authors do not need another random service quote. They need someone to tell them what stage the manuscript is really in, what files they need, and what order the work should happen in.

What This Solves

Clear Direction Before
You Spend on the Wrong Thing

This consultation is for authors who are stuck between proofreading, formatting, cover design, KDP, IngramSpark, eBook conversion, and platform questions. We review the stage of the manuscript and map the practical path forward so you do not guess your way through publishing decisions.

Manuscript stage assessment
Recommended publishing path based on the book type
File-format clarity for print and eBook versions
Guidance on KDP versus IngramSpark when relevant
Clear next-step recommendation before production begins

Best For

First-time authors who are unsure what service comes first or what the book actually needs before it is professionally publishable.

Why It Matters

Authors often waste time and money by buying cover design before proofreading, or formatting before the manuscript is stable. This step prevents that.

Next Step

After the review, you get a clearer recommendation on whether the project belongs in Starter Formatting, Author Launch Bundle, Premium Publishing Kit, or a custom quote.

Consultation Questions

Publishing Consultation FAQ

What if I do not know whether I need proofreading or formatting first?
That is exactly the kind of issue the consultation is for. We review the manuscript stage and recommend the correct order.
Can you tell me whether I should use KDP, IngramSpark, or both?
Yes. We can explain the practical tradeoffs in terms of distribution goals, production complexity, and what your book is trying to do.
Is this only for authors who already want to hire Literary Lab?
No. The consultation itself is meant to create clarity first. If there is a fit afterward, the service path becomes much easier to scope properly.
