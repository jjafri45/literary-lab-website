Proofreading

Proofreading Before Design
Makes the Whole Book Stronger

Formatting does not fix grammar, punctuation, or consistency problems. Proofreading first helps the book read more cleanly and reduces friction later in production.

What You Get

A Cleaner Manuscript
Before Layout Starts

This service is designed for authors whose manuscript is largely written but still needs a cleanup pass before the design phase. That includes grammar, punctuation, spelling, and consistency issues that affect how professional the final book feels.

Line-by-line proofreading
Tracked changes in Word format
Grammar, spelling, and punctuation corrections
Consistency checks for names, headings, and recurring terms
One revision round for follow-up fixes

Best For

Authors who finished the manuscript draft but do not want obvious surface-level errors carried into the final formatted book.

Not Included

Deep developmental editing, structural rewrites, and ghostwriting are outside the base proofreading scope.

Starting Price

$49 for standard manuscripts up to the listed scope. Heavier cleanup or larger manuscripts may require a custom quote.

Author Questions

Proofreading FAQ

Do I need proofreading before formatting?
Usually yes. A cleaner manuscript produces a cleaner final book and reduces the need to reopen design files for avoidable text corrections later.
Is proofreading the same as editing?
No. Proofreading focuses on surface-level corrections like grammar, punctuation, and consistency. Heavier structural or developmental editing is a different service level.
Will I receive tracked changes?
Yes. The manuscript is returned with tracked changes so you can review what was corrected before it moves into design or publishing prep.
