Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does a project take?
Most interior formatting projects are completed in 3–5 business days. Cover design typically takes 4–7 business days. Rush turnaround (24–48 hours) is available for an additional fee — just mention it in your inquiry.
How many revisions are included?
Revision rounds are included within the approved project scope. If the direction changes materially after approval, we clarify the added work before continuing so expectations stay clear on both sides.
What format should I send my manuscript in?
We accept Microsoft Word (.docx), Google Docs (exported as .docx), or plain text files. Don't worry about formatting it perfectly — that's our job. Just make sure the content is proofread and final.
Do you provide the print-ready PDF?
Yes. Every project includes a press-ready PDF with correct bleed, margins, and embedded fonts — ready to upload directly to KDP, IngramSpark, or your chosen print platform. We also provide the source InDesign file on request.
How do I pay?
We accept PayPal, Wise (TransferWise), and bank transfer. A 50% deposit is required to start, with the remainder due upon delivery. We also accept payment through Upwork for clients who prefer that platform.
Can I see samples of your interior formatting?
Absolutely. Just ask on WhatsApp or in your inquiry form and we'll send you sample pages from projects in your genre. We have samples for fiction, non-fiction, workbooks, and more.
